About Us

Building Character, Creating Leaders

Established in 1995, Adarsh Pragati Hr. Sec. School has been a beacon of excellence in education for over two decades. Our vision is to nurture young minds into becoming responsible global citizens with strong Indian values.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual curiosity, creativity, and moral values in students, preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow.
